ABOUT US

Taking Divine Power to the Nations

Christ Ambassadors Ministries (All Nations Worship Center) is a global ministry with a vision of taking God's divine power to the nations of the world and demonstrating the power of the resurrected Christ.

Through the years, various arms of the Ministry have been birthed to effectively proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ. The Ministry has a vibrant TV Ministry, Internet Ministry, healing programs, and outreach missions.

OUR LEADERSHIP

Meet Our Spiritual Leaders

Resident Pastor

Pastor Lawrence Whyte

Serving alongside our Apostle in this divine calling, I am committed to shepherding God's flock with wisdom, compassion, and the power of the Holy Spirit. Together, we labor to build disciples, strengthen families, equip believers for ministry, and advance the kingdom of God in our community and beyond. Our hearts are set on seeing every believer walk in the fullness of their divine inheritance in Christ.

Founder & Apostle

Apostle Innocent Edoh

God has ordained me as an Apostle with a special unction - a gift of the Holy Spirit to perform and fulfill His divine purpose in my life. Called to preach the gospel to the poor, heal the brokenhearted, preach deliverance to the captives, and set at liberty those who are bruised. Our mission is to reconcile the whole world unto God through Christ Jesus, demonstrating the power of the resurrected Christ to all nations.
